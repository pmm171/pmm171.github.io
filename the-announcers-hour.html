TAH #20 – Gavin Bicho, Sports Director WVUM 90.5 FM

In the 20th episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Gavin Bicho joins the podcast to discuss his role as the Sports Director of WVUM 90.5 FM.

In his current role, Bicho serves as the Sports Director for the University of Miami’s student-run radio station, WVUM 90.5 FM. Bicho is also a regular contributor to the university’s television program, UMTV.

Bicho considered staying in Rhode Island before ultimately choosing Miami. There, Bicho covered Rhode Island Rams athletics and continues to do so as a contributor to A10Talk.
