TAH #21 – Scot Gladstone, Glacier Range Riders, Washington

In the 21st episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Scot Gladstone joins the podcast to discuss his roles broadcasting in baseball and college athletics.

Gladstone is the voice of the Glacier Range Riders, a professional baseball team in the Pioneer League, which is a partner league of Major League Baseball. Gladstone is also a consistent voice of athletic broadcasts for the University of Washington in Seattle.

Gladstone is from Montana and spent time with the Rockford Rivets of the Northwoods League and the Bristol Blues in the New England Collegiate Baseball League. Gladstone was also a broadcaster for North Park and Saint Martin’s University and graduated from Colorado College.
