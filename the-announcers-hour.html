TAH #22 – Stephen Buxton, Palm Springs Power, California Winter League

In the 22nd episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Stephen Buxton joins us to talk about his experience with the California Winter League and Palm Springs Power. Buxton also talks about his time at Arizona State University.

Buxton is an alum of Walter Cronkite’s School of Journalism and Mass Communication at Arizona State University. Buxton spent the last two years calling games for the Palm Springs Power and the California Winter League. Buxton has also called several high school events.
