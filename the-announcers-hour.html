TAH #23 – Trey Weston, Lipscomb, Maryville

In the 23rd episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Trey Weston joins the pod to discuss his broadcasting journey in university and beyond.

Weston attend Lipscomb University in Nashville, Tennessee and called several sports for the student run radio station "The Bison" and ESPN+. He continues to call games for Lipscomb on ESPN+, and now participates as a broadcaster for several sports at Maryville University in St. Louis, Missouri.
