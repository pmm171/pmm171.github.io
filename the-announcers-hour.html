TAH #11 – Knight Jarecki, KSKR, Springfield Drifters

In the 11th episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Knight Jarecki joins me to chat about his time at the University of Oregon and his duties as the voice of the Springfield Drifters in the West Coast League.

Jarecki is a Sports/News Broadcaster with Brooke Communications (KSKR - 92.3 FM, 1490 AM The Score) and the play-by-play voice of the WCL’s Springfield Drifters.

With a love for the Pacific Northwest, Jarecki graduated from the University of Oregon’s Journalism school. At UO, Jarecki served as the Sports Director of KWVA 88.1 FM, Oregon’s student run radio station. In addition to serving as a broadcaster for the station, Pac-12 Network, ESPN+, OSAA, and the Conejo Oaks, the Oregon graduate created articles on Oregon athletics and games.
