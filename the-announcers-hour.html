TAH #19 – Spencer McLaughlin, Voice of Southern Utah Thunderbirds

In the 19th episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Spencer McLaughlin joins the podcast to discuss his role as the Voice of the Southern Utah Thunderbirds.

McLaughlin is the play-by-play voice of Southern Utah football and men’s basketball. In addition, McLaughlin is a podcast host for the “Locked On” network and covers the Oregon Ducks. Before taking on his current roles, McLaughlin was the radio and television voice of Eastern Oklahoma State College.

McLaughlin graduated from Santa Clara University in 2020. While there, McLaughlin was a student broadcaster for KSCU 103.3 FM. During his senior year, McLaughlin served as the station’s Sports Director.
