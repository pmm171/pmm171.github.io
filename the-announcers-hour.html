Episode 5 - Rich Hollenberg, ESPN Play-by-Play, Tampa Bay Rays Pre/Postgame

In the fifth episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Rich Hollenberg joins me to discuss his long and prosperous broadcasting career. Hollenberg has been long renowned for his versatility and multi-talented skillset in sports broadcasting.

Hollenberg is currently working as a Studio Host and contributor to the Tampa Bay Rays television broadcasts on Bally Sports. Rich gets on the call for college basketball play-by-play with ESPN and is most often involved in the Big 12 conference.

Hollenberg has been all over sports and media since his inception into the business out of Syracuse University’s famous S.I. Newhouse School. Hollenberg has been featured many times on both the MLB Network and NFL Network.
