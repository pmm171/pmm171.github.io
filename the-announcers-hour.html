TAH #15 – Joel Norman, Wenatchee AppleSox, Lincoln Stars

In the 15th episode of “The Announcer’s Hour” podcast, Joel Norman joins me to talk about broadcasting in the West Coast League (WCL) and the United States Hockey League (USHL).

Norman is the current voice of the Wenatchee AppleSox in the WCL. In the baseball offseason, Norman has play-by-play duties for the Lincoln Stars of the USHL.

Norman graduated from West Virginia University and started in his baseball path by calling Mountaineers baseball. In the first part of Norman’s stint in Wenatchee, the broadcaster worked on the sales side and helped the AppleSox grow. In late August, Norman was named the WCL Broadcaster of the Year.
