Patrick McColl - Sportscaster

About Patrick

Patrick McColl is a graduate of the University of Miami with a B.S.C in Broadcast Journalism and a minor in Sports Administration. He currently serves as the voice of the Ridgefield Raptors in the West Coast League.

Patrick is a play-by-play broadcaster with a passion for sports broadcasting and media.

You can find day-to-day updates on what Patrick is doing on his social media pages. Follow on Twitter, YouTube, and LinkedIn.

Experience

Patrick just finished his second season with the Ridgefield Raptors in the West Coast League. Beforehand, Patrick was with KSJB AM 600 and MIX 93.3 FM in Jamestown, ND, serving as a Show Host, Play-by-Play Commentator, Production Manager, and Assistant Engineer.

With WVUM 90.5 FM, Miami's student radio station, Patrick had play-by-play duties for several Hurricanes games, calling football, baseball, men's and women's basketball, volleyball, and soccer.

In time with WGEM and UMTV, Patrick has years of television experience, working both in front and behind the camera.

Patrick has sports articles published in the Miami Herald, Miami Hurricane, and Parkway Central High School Corral. Patrick has also contributed to the sports website FanBuzz.com.
