Super Bowl 57 - Patrick McColl

On Sunday, Glendale Arizona will host the 57th Super Bowl in the National Football League's history. This game will decide the NFL champion for the 2022 NFL season.

The game will be played between the AFC champion Kansas City Chiefs and the NFC champion Philadelphia Eagles.

Sportsbooks currently price the game as follows:

  • Point Spread - PHI -1.5 (-110)
  • Over/Under - 51 total points (-110)
  • Moneyline - KC (+105) / PHI (-125)

While these two teams are set for a competetive clash, it was hardly an easy road to get to this point. Both the Chiefs and the Eagles won the top seeds in their respective conferences.

Chiefs Postseason

Kansas City finished 14-3 in the regular season and won the first overall seed in the AFC, receiving a bye week during Wild Card Weekend.

Divisional Round - vs. Jaguars (27-20) Win

The Chiefs led early and late against the Jaguars. Despite a high ankle injury halfway through the game, the Chiefs starting quarterback Patrick Mahomes threw for 195 yards and two touchdowns.

AFC Championship - vs. Bengals (23-20) Win

In a back and forth game against the team that knocked them out last year, the Chiefs put together a four play, 26 yard scoring drive to kick a 45 yard field goal and take the lead with just three seconds left in the game.

Eagles Postseason

Philadelphia finished 14-3 in the regular season and won the first overall seed in the NFC, receiving a bye week during Wild Card Weekend.

Divisional Round - vs. Giants (38-7) Win

After the Giants pulled off the upset in Minnesota, they were solemly defeated in Philadelphia. The Eagles outgained the Giants by 189 yards.

NFC Championship - vs. 49ers (31-7) Win

Winning via blowout the second straight week, the Eagles took advantage of the 49ers quarterback injuries and cruised to a victory. The Eagles rushed for 148 yards on the ground.
